In the Time of COVID January 2020 - March 2021


This prototype visualization prototype uses shapes and color to illustrate the meaningful experiences of individuals who confidentially volunteered their memories from the COVID-19 era (January 2020 - March 2021). Reading across from left to right then top to bottom, each date within this period is represented as a SARS-CoV-2 glycoprotein. Dates (SARS-CoV-2 glycoproteins) filled with color depict a color that the participant selected as beign representative of their COVID-era memory. The dots located within the glycoprotein represent the occurrence of an historical COVID-related event. And the total number of confirmed global cases and global deaths for each date is located below the glycoprotein. Hover over the shapes to learn more about what happened on that date.
Data on COVID events from Wikipedia. Data on global confirmed cases and deaths from the COVID-19 Data Repository by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins UniversityData of COVID-era memories sourced through online survey.
